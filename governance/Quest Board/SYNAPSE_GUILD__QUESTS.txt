SYNAPSE GUILD — QUESTS
Version: v2.0
Last Updated: 2026-03-31

================================================================================
1) WHAT A QUEST IS
================================================================================
A Quest is a bounded coherent outcome that can be accepted, executed, audited, and closed honestly.

A Quest is not:
- the smallest atomic task
- a disposable to-do item
- a temporary plan scratchpad

Atomic work belongs inside milestones and work items.

================================================================================
2) QUEST STATE MACHINE
================================================================================
- BOARD
- ACCEPTED
- COMPLETED
- ABANDONED

State is determined by filesystem location, not prose claims.

================================================================================
3) QUESTS AND PLANS
================================================================================
Execution-ready plans must be persisted under .synapse/PLANS/.
A plan revision may map to one quest or multiple linked quests depending on closure surfaces.

Rules:
- one coherent outcome may have many milestones and still be one quest
- multiple independently closable outcomes must split into multiple quests
- quests must link back to their plan revision(s)

================================================================================
4) ACCEPTANCE VS COMPLETION
================================================================================
Acceptance means:
- the quest is lawfully ready for governed execution
- the audit bundle exists
- verification plan is concrete

Completion means:
- the latest completion audit is a clean PASS
- the final state decision is COMPLETED
- downstream continuity truth may now treat the quest as completed

================================================================================
5) AUDIT MODEL
================================================================================
New-write quest bundles use:
- 00_SUMMARY.md
- 01_COMPLETION_AUDIT.md
- append-only numbered completion-audit history when later attempts supersede earlier ones
- 06_CHANGED_FILES.txt
- 06_TESTS.txt
- 06_WRAPPER_PROOF.json

Historical 4-phase bundles remain readable as legacy history.

================================================================================
6) TALENT LAW
================================================================================
A quest may award a Talent Point only when it expands real project capability.
Non-capability work may still be valid quest work; it simply records Talent Point Awarded: NO.

================================================================================
7) SNAPSHOTS AND QUESTS
================================================================================
Snapshots summarize continuity.
They do not replace quest audits.
If a snapshot claims quest completion, it must point to the quest’s real completion audit.

END OF DOCUMENT
