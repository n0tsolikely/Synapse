RESPEC RULES — SYNAPSE GUILD
Purpose: Respec is the ceremonial, explicit process for reversing or changing a previously spent Talent Point.
Rule: Respec is rare. It must be intentional, logged, and consequence-aware.

Requirements:
1) Must be done during a Control Sync.
2) Must state:
   - Talent removed/changed (TALENT ID)
   - Reason
   - Consequences (what breaks, what changes)
   - Migration plan (if needed)
3) Must be logged in TALENT LOG as a respec event (append-only).

Respec event format (append-only, log into TALENT LOG):
YYYY-MM-DD
