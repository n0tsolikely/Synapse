# TERM — Dailies

## Definition
Dailies are the execution loop used to perform Quests.

## Purpose
Dailies standardize how work is executed, tested, verified, and recorded so progress is reliable and repeatable.

## Typical Loop
- Decide Scope (select/accept quests)
- Implement
- Test
- Snapshot (and audit updates)
- Stop / Resume

## Do Not Assume
- Dailies are not Control Syncs
- Dailies do not create strategy
- Dailies do not auto-select Quests

## Interactions
- Dailies execute Quests during a Raid
- Results feed into End-of-Day snapshots and audits
