# TERM — Guild Orders

## Definition
Guild Orders are strategic commitments selecting which portions of the Codex/Map will be executed (scope at an objective level).

## Purpose
Guild Orders prevent opportunistic drift by making scope explicit before execution.

## When It Applies
- After Codex Freeze (Fog Lifted)
- Before Raids and Quest generation

## What It Enables
- Raid creation
- Dungeon partitioning
- Focused execution toward defined outcomes

## Do Not Assume
- Guild Orders are not execution
- Guild Orders are not atomic tasks
- Guild Orders do not guarantee completion

## Interactions
- Guild Orders reference Codex sections
- Raids execute Guild Orders
