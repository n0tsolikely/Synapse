# TERM — Dungeon

## Definition
A Dungeon is a logical subdivision of a Raid that groups related Quests.

## Purpose
Dungeons break complex execution into manageable sections without losing alignment to the Raid objective.

## When It Applies
- During Raid planning and execution
- When Quests share dependencies/context

## What It Enables
- Staged execution
- Reduced cognitive load
- Clear progress tracking

## Do Not Assume
- A Dungeon is not a Raid
- A Dungeon is not an atomic unit

## Interactions
- Dungeons belong to Raids
- Dungeons contain Quests
