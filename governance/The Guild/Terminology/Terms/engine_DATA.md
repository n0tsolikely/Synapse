# TERM — Engine + Data

## Definition
- **Engine** = the deliverable workspace (the thing being built).
- **Data** = the continuity + governance artifacts that describe, constrain, and audit the work.

## In Synapse OS itself
- The folder you drop into AIs (this pack) is the **portable Engine pack**: `Synapse_OS_Governance/...`
  - It is stateless canon (laws + processes + terminology).
- The companion continuity pack, when used, is **Data**: `Synapse_OS_Data/...`

## In any Subject
- `<Subject>_Engine/` contains the subject itself (codebase, manuscript, game project, etc.).
- `<Subject>_Data/` contains Codex, Snapshots, Guild Orders, Quest Board, Audits, Buffs, etc.

## Rule of separation
- Engine is where the product lives.
- Data is where the map, orders, audits, and continuity live.

## Notes
Eventually, Synapse OS may grow a true runtime Engine. Until then, the Governance Pack is the portable Engine for governing AI behavior.
