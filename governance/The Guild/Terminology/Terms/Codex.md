# TERM — Codex (Map)

## Definition
The Codex is the authoritative structural representation of a Subject: what exists, where it lives, how parts relate, and what is allowed.

**Alias (conversation):** The Codex may be referred to as the **Map**.

## Purpose
The Codex eliminates ambiguity before execution by freezing an agreed structure and constraint set.

## When It Applies
- After sufficient Discoveries have accumulated
- When Fog of War is ready to be lifted
- Before Guild Orders / Quests may be generated safely

## What It Enables
- Scope selection via Guild Orders
- Quest generation and execution planning
- Deterministic “painting by numbers” implementation

## Do Not Assume
- The Codex is not execution
- The Codex is not a snapshot/log
- The Codex does not change implicitly
- The Codex does not guarantee correctness of implementation—only structure and intent

## Interactions
- Codex Freeze = Fog Lifted (World State transition)
- Codex sections are referenced by Guild Orders and Dungeons
- Codex boundaries constrain Quests
