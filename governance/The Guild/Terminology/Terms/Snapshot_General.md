# TERM — General Snapshot

## Definition
A General Snapshot is an ad-hoc continuity capture that does not imply formal alignment or execution.

## Purpose
It preserves context when neither a Control Sync Snapshot nor an End-of-Day Snapshot is appropriate.

## Do Not Assume
- It is not a Control Sync
- It is not an execution receipt

## Interactions
- May be referenced during Control Sync to recover context
