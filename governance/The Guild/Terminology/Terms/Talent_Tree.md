# TERM — Talent Tree

## Definition
The Talent Tree is a high-level capability index: what the system can do now, based on completed Quests.

## Purpose
Talents reduce rehydration cost by summarizing capabilities without requiring rereading full history or code.

## What It Enables
- Fast capability awareness for new operators or AIs
- Easier planning of expansions and next Guild Orders

## Do Not Assume
- Talents are not replacements for receipts
- Talents do not guarantee quality
- Talents are an index, not the full proof

## Interactions
- Quests award Talents
- Talents point back to receipts (snapshots/audits) as needed
