# Incubation

**Definition (routing term):**  
Incubation is the **pre-Codex** phase where the Subject does not yet have an authoritative Map (Codex).

During Incubation:
- the system is operating under **Fog of War** by default
- exploration and brainstorming are allowed
- the system captures concrete decisions and emerging structure as **Discoveries**
- execution (Quests / Raids) is gated until **Codex Freeze (Fog Lifted)** unless Hands explicitly overrides

**Purpose:**  
Prevent premature execution and refactors by forcing the idea to crystallize before building.
