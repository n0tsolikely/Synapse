# TERM — Quest (including Side Quests)

## Definition
A Quest is an atomic unit of work with a clear start, clear end, and evidence requirement.

A Side Quest is a Quest that emerges outside the main planned line of work (e.g., a bug found during testing, a small improvement discovered mid-raid).

## Purpose
Quests make work executable, auditable, and bounded. Side Quests allow real-world discoveries to be captured without derailing the main plan.

## When It Applies
- After Codex Freeze (Fog Lifted)
- Under an active Guild Order / Raid
- When new issues or opportunities are discovered during execution

## What It Enables
- Precise execution
- Evidence-based completion
- Capability unlock recording (Talents)

## Do Not Assume
- A Quest is not discussion
- A Quest is not vague aspiration
- A Quest is not complete without evidence

## Interactions
- Quests are executed via Dailies
- Quests belong to Dungeons
- Quests can be promoted/paused/abandoned with recorded reasons
