# TERM — Truth Gate

## Definition
Truth Gate enforces that claims about actions, artifacts, or state must be supported by evidence.

## Purpose
Truth Gate prevents hallucination, false completion, and narrative drift.

## When It Applies
- Before claiming execution occurred
- Before marking Quests complete
- When referencing artifacts that may or may not exist

## Do Not Assume
- Truth Gate does not spam confirmations when things are fine
- Truth Gate does not guarantee correctness by itself
- Truth Gate does not replace audits; it requires them

## Interactions
- Truth Gate requires audit receipts
- Truth Gate pairs with Disclosure Gate when divergences occur
