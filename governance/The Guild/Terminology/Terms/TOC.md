# TERM — Table of Contents (Legend)

## Definition
The Table of Contents (TOC) is the navigational index of the Codex.

**Alias (conversation):** The TOC may be referred to as the **Legend**.

## Purpose
The TOC makes the Map navigable and defines the layout before full Codex content is written.

## When It Applies
- During Codex formation
- Before or during Codex writing
- When referencing Codex structure and scope

## What It Enables
- Section-by-section Codex construction
- Clear reference points for Guild Orders and Dungeons

## Do Not Assume
- The TOC is not execution-ready
- The TOC is not a commitment to implement everything immediately
- The TOC does not imply completeness

## Interactions
- TOC entries map to Codex sections
- Guild Orders reference TOC/Codex sections
